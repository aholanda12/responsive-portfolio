Lemon Demon Stans Unite!

I'm Sorry, Who?

On the wikipedia page for Lemon Demon, they're listed as a "musical project" before they're listed as a band, which isn't an unfair assessment. Lemon Demon is the brainchild of one Neil Cicierega, an artist who internet enthusiasts may also know for his other projects like Potter Puppet Pals and Animutation.

Still not ringing any bells? That's fair. It's all pretty niche and honestly kind of weird, but that's the appeal of it. I have been told in the past that my taste of music is "garbage" and "terrible" and "give me the AUX cord if you aren't going to play anything good" but I implore you to open your mind and ears to the joy that is Lemon Demon.
